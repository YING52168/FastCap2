FastCap2 User Guide

Welcome to FastCap2 !

FastCap2 is a powerful three-dimensional capactiance extraction program.

Features include:

  • Computation of self and mutual capacitances between ideal conductors of arbitrary shapes, orientations and sizes!
  • Dielectric regions composed if any number of constant-permittivity regions of any shape and size!
  • Very fast solution!
  • Full automation support allowing remote control of FastCap2 by other programs, either in foreground or in background!
  • Lots of options!
  • Free (see disclaimer and conditions of use)!
